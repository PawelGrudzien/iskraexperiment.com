> M.R.R.R. - CROWDSOURCED VOICE RECORDING CAMPAIGN

> Hey all, I am a sound artist and composer Pawel Grudzien and I'm looking for some crowdsourced voice recordings for my new experimental project [is.kr/a]. For examples of my previous work please visit the home page and my personal website www.pawelgrudzien.com.

> I am looking for as many voice recordings as possible of the following text for my new audiovisual work (working title M.R.R.R.):

"What we see now is like a dim image in a mirror.
Then we shall see face to face."

> (it is a quote from the Bible - 1 Cor 13:12, also famously used in the amazing Japanese film Ghost in the shell).

> The recording needs to be quite slow, the whole sentence should be about 12 seconds long. It does not have to be studio quality, but please try to keep the background noise to minimum. All types of recording accepted: use your phone's voice memo app, your laptop's mic ( Audacity is a good app to use), professional condenser mic or whatever else you have. Extra points for recordings on the old tape dictaphones or using any other old technology. I prefer WAV or MP3 file format (although all other popular audio formats will be accepted too). Feel free to send a few versions if you feel like it :D

> You will be credited in the track artwork/description.

> Upload the file here.

> You don't need to sign up to dropbox to upload. Just select the file and enter your name and email. I tried to keep the upload process as simple as possible, if you have any difficulty please message me through my facebook page fb.me/iskraexperiment. It goes without saying that I will never use your email address for anything else other than the communication and updates regarding the [is.kr/a] project.

> The deadline for submissions is 28 February 2017.

> The track and the video will be released for free on soundcloud, bandcamp and youtube in March under Creative Commons license (free for anyone to download and remix). I will send you an email notification once this happens. I am also considering a release on iTunes/Spotify/Google Music and similar services.

> Please share this page to spread the word. For new music and to get the most up to date information about this and my other projects please follow [is.kr/a] using the links below.

> Thank you!

> All the best,
Pawel
